Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages (TESOL)

On July 6, Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages (TESOL) master's program, a joint program of NYU Shanghai and the NYU Steinhardt School...

On July 6, the TESOL master's program, a collaborative initiative between NYU Shanghai and the prestigious NYU Steinhardt School of Culture, Education, and Human Development, officially commenced. This unique program was designed to equip aspiring educators with the advanced skills and knowledge required to teach English to speakers of other languages in a variety of global contexts. By combining the expertise and resources of both NYU Shanghai and NYU Steinhardt, the program offers students a rich and dynamic learning environment, where they can gain both theoretical insights and practical teaching experience. Through immersive coursework, cutting-edge research opportunities, and hands-on teaching practicums, participants are trained to become leaders in the field of TESOL, capable of addressing the challenges and demands of language education in an increasingly interconnected world.
